Hands Ranking
//@Dominic rules retrieved from RIO GRANDE'S manual or FATA MORGANA SPIELE, Bern and made partially made up examples.
1. A Sequence of length of at least five. E.g. 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8.
2. A full house (trio & pair). E.g. 3, 3, 3, 9, 9.
3. A trio of cards of equal rank. E.g. 9, 9, 9.
4. A sequence of pairs of adjacent value. E.g. J, J, Q, Q, K, K.
5. A pair of cars of equal rank. E.g. 9, 9
6. A single card. E.g. 9
