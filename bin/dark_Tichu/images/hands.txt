Hands Ranking

1. Royal Flush: consist of the ace, king, queen, jack and ten of a single suit.
2. Straight Flush: Five cards in a sequence, all of the same suit.
3. Four of a Kind: Four cards of the same rank and one side card.
4. Full House: Three cards of the same rank and two cards of a different matching rank.
5. Flush: Five cards of the same suit, but not in sequence.
6. Straight: Five cards in sequence, but not of the same suit.
7. Three of a Kind: Three cards of the same rank and two unrelated side cards.
8. Two Pair: Two different pairs.
9. Pair: Two cards of matching rank and three unrelated side cards. 
10. High Card: Any hand that does not qualify under the categories listed.
